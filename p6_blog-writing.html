Challenge/Problem



Clearly faced the challenge of low organic traffic to its e-commerce platform. The company struggled with SEO rankings, hindering its ability to reach a broader audience and compete effectively in the online eyewear market. High volumes of customer support calls were also placing a strain on resources, indicating a need to proactively address customer queries and concerns through alternative means.


My Role & Process



My role involved driving three blog sites for Clearly in Canada, Australia, and New Zealand, and crafting compelling and informative blogs to address the identified challenges. The process included:

  • 1.

    Market research


    Conduct in-depth research to understand the target audience, industry trends, content ideas, and relevant keywords for SEO optimization.

  • 2.

    Content strategy development


    Create a comprehensive content strategy that aligns seamlessly with overall brand messaging and strategic marketing and SEO goals, emphasizing customer education and engagement.

  • 3.

    Collaboration


    Work closely with the marketing and customer support teams to identify key topics and pain points, ensuring content relevance and effectiveness.

  • 4.

    Content calendar management


    Develop and maintain a structured content calendar to ensure a consistent flow of blogs aligned with seasonal trends, product launches, and marketing initiatives. Helps optimize content timing and relevance, maximizing its impact on audience engagement.

  • 5.

    Content optimization


    Optimize content based on performance and SEO data for various content elements, including keywords, headlines, content, images, CTAs, and formatting so that content remains engaging to the target audience's search queries and search intent.

  • 6.

    Content promotion


    Strategically increase the visibility and reach of blog content to a target audience through social media platforms, email marketing, and internal linking.

Solution



Leveraged strategically chosen keywords into blog content to enhance SEO performance and increase organic search traffic.

Created educational content that proactively addressed common customer queries, reducing the frequency of customer support calls and enhancing the overall customer experience.

Produced engaging and shareable content for marketing campaign integration to maximize reach and engagement.

Prioritized top-performing blogs and implemented SEO on-site optimization techniques to improve its visibility, engagement, and effectiveness.
