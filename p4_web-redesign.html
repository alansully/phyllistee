Challenge/Problem



Clearly presented us with the challenge of redesigning their e-commerce website, and migrating all of their website content pages with limited resources and setting lots of timeline-driven restrictions. This is the first time Clearly has ever redesigned their 23-year-old website in Canada, Australia, and New Zealand.

My Role & Process



My role as a lead writer was to craft compelling, user-centric, and mobile-first content that will elevate the overall user experience, ensuring seamless navigation, clear communication, and increased conversion rates.

  • 1.

    Stakeholder meetings


    Document, prioritize, and agree upon the website redesign requirements including landing pages, search bar, and calls-to-action, as well as SEO and UX copy requirements.

  • 2.

    Content audit


    Review content quality on the "old" website to gain insight into content gaps: which content to create, update, re-write, or delete, and ensure a streamlined and cohesive narrative.

  • 3.

    Content templates


    Provide a structured framework that contributes to efficient and effective content creation across various different content types, facilitates collaboration, scales content production, optimizes for UX and SEO, and enables a basis for measurable success.

  • 4.

    Copy development


    Produce high-quality, effective content that resonates with the target audience, page intent, placement of copy, and Google search ranking factors while advancing Clearly’s brand voice.

  • 5.

    Content localization


    Adapt content to suit local audiences in three countries, ensuring a personalized and relatable experience by identifying words and phrases that are commonly spoken in the respective countries.

  • 6.

    Collaborative design


    Work closely with the design team to integrate UX writing seamlessly into the visual elements, creating a harmonious user journey.

Solution



Conducted a thorough content audit and implemented effective content solutions for enhancing the overall content quality, relevance, and performance to help visitors easily navigate the redesigned website.

By creating and following a content template, we were able to make sure every content follows a specific format and makes it easier for our team of designers and developers to publish it onto the redesigned website.

Used plain language, persuasive and intuitive content - from page titles to footer copies - to help users make important decisions when interacting with the website while maintaining consistent messaging and clear communication that aligns with the Clearly brand.

Refined microcopy across the website, including user flows, CTAs, error messages, modals, forms, and tooltips, for a more user-friendly interaction.
